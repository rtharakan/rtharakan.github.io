Hi, I'm Robin.

I am based in Brooklyn, NY. The proper name of this city is the Munsee word, Lenapehoking, which means “Land of the Lenape.” The land where I live, play, and explore is stolen from the Indigenous People who first loved it and cared for it.

I am here by way of Boston (colonized Wôpanâak (Wampanoag) and Massachusett Tribe traditional territory), New Delhi, and Pune. I'm a Malayalee (a speaker of Malayalam) and spent my formative years between Bahrain and the South Indian state, Kerala.

About this site.

I do not have a name for this website (yet). This personal website project is inspired by many online spaces I spent countless hours on - collections of essays and marginalia, observations and critiques, memories and visions. I find immense joy in reading stories and dispatches from friends and strangers.
